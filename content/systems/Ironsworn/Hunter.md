# [[Hunter]]

> [!info] Hunter
> **Rank:**
> 
> Dangerous (2 progress per harm; inflicts 2 harm)
> 
> **Traits:**
> 
> - Wearing hides and furs to ward away the cold
> - Steely gaze
> - At home in the woodlands
> 
> **Drives:**
> 
> - A clean kill
> - Survive the hunt
> 
> **Tactics:**
> 
> - Set traps
> - Keep to the shadows
> - Deadly shot
